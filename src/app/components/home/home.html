Darkstar Created by Victor Kane

Darkstar is a powerful tool designed for text obfuscation and deobfuscation. It provides a suite of cryptographic and non-cryptographic methods to transform your text, making it unreadable to unauthorized users while allowing for easy reversal for those with the key.

Features:

AES-256 Encryption: Secure your text with a strong, industry-standard encryption algorithm. Multiple Obfuscation Layers: Apply a variety of techniques like reversing, character encoding, and ciphers. Flexible Deobfuscation: The application can intelligently reverse the obfuscation steps to recover the original text. Dark/Light Theme: Choose the visual mode that's most comfortable for you.
