About Darkstar

Description

A client-side security tool to safeguard your Bitcoin wallet's recovery phrase (BIP39 mnemonic). It provides a robust, multi-layered solution for obfuscating and encrypting your seed phrase, adding a critical layer of security to protect your digital assets.

Key Features

wifi_off

Secure Offline Encryption

All encryption and decryption happens entirely on your device. No data ever leaves your machine, ensuring complete privacy.

key

BIP39 Mnemonic Support

Full support for 12 and 24-word recovery phrases following the BIP39 standard used by most hardware and software wallets.

security

Anti-Forensic Memory Protection

Sensitive data is cleared from memory after use, reducing the risk of recovery through forensic analysis.

devices

Cross-Platform Compatibility

Available for Windows, macOS, and Linux. Your encrypted seed can be decrypted on any supported platform.

layers

Multi-Layer Obfuscation

Combines word shuffling, decoy injection, and strong encryption to create multiple layers of protection.

verified_user

Open Source & Auditable

Fully open-source codebase. Review the code yourself or have it audited by security professionals.
